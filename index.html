About the Bodhi School of Yoga

Where Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Science

The Bodhi School of Yoga is a renowned yoga institution committed to nurturing yoga as a complete way of life — not merely a physical exercise. With studios across India in Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Vizag, and Goa, Bodhi creates an ashram-style environment that is simple, sacred, and soul-nourishing.

Their mission is powerful: to awaken one lakh (100,000) yoga teachers across the world who are spiritually rooted, scientifically trained, and emotionally empowered. Their programs are aligned with Yoga Alliance (USA) standards, ensuring globally recognized certification.

Bodhi's comprehensive curriculum integrates traditional yogic wisdom — including the Patanjali Yoga Sutras and Bhagavad Gita — with modern anatomy, physiology, and evidence-based practices, producing teachers who are both deeply grounded in tradition and equipped for the contemporary world.
